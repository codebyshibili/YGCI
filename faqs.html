About YGCI

Yatha Green Council in India (YGCI) is an environmental initiative based in Tamil Nadu focused on restoration, education, and sustainability.

YGCI stands for Yatha Green Council in India, a community-driven green movement.

We are headquartered in Tamil Nadu and operate in both urban and rural communities across the state.
